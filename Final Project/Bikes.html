Rental Requirements

To ensure a smooth rental process, please review the following requirements:

  1. Driving License: Provide a copy of your valid motorcycle driving license.
  2. Passport/ID: Submit a copy of your passport or national identity card.
  3. Prepayment & Deposit: Full rental fee prepayment and a minimum deposit of Ksh5,000-Ksh50,000 are required (depending on the bike).
  4. Accommodation Details: Let us know where you will be staying for ease of drop-off/pick-up.
  5. Rental Period: Our minimum rental period is 2 days, with no maximum limit.
  6. Discounts: We offer discounts for long-term rentals, subject to negotiation.
  7. Border Charge: Additional charges apply for crossing borders with our bikes.
